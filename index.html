DreamWhisper Privacy Policy

Last updated: December 2025

DreamWhisper is a bedtime story app that runs entirely on your device.

Data We Store Locally

  • Character names you enter for story personalization
  • Story preferences and settings
  • Generated stories and history
  • AI model files (downloaded once for offline use)

Data We Do Not Collect

  • We do not collect personal information
  • We do not track your usage or behavior
  • We do not send any data to external servers
  • All AI processing happens locally on your device

Payments and Subscriptions

Subscription payments are processed by the respective app store (Apple App Store or Google Play). We do not collect, store, or have access to your payment information. Subscription management and cancellation can be done through your device's app store settings.

AI-Generated Content

Stories are generated using Google's Gemma AI model running locally on your device. Generated content is not reviewed, stored, or transmitted by us.

Data Deletion

Uninstalling the app removes all stored data from your device.
